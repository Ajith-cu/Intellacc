    ABOUT US

    We are rendering comprehensive professional services in the field of Business Formation and Post Incorporation Services, GST and Income Tax Services, Accounting and Auditing Services, Start-up Registration and Advisory Services, Management Consultancy Services,Legal and Corporate Secretarial Services, Project financing and Managerial aspects etc.

    In growing economy new MSME business and start-up is setting up rapidly, in changing scenario tax and compliances are also revamping its pace and people are in need of good Professional Services. We are a team of Chartered Accountant, Company Secretary, Cost and Management Accountant, Advocate, GST Practitioner in Kerala and committed to provide best professional services to our clients to overcome the complexities in law and compliances hence they can concentrate their core business activities.
